Frequently Asked Questions

What's the typical timeline for a design project?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Residential projects typically take 3-6 months for design development, while commercial projects may require 6-12 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work on projects outside New York?

Yes, we take on select projects nationwide and internationally. For projects outside the NYC area, we collaborate with local contractors and may charge additional travel expenses for site visits.

What's included in your design services?

Our comprehensive services include concept development, detailed drawings, material selection, 3D visualizations, permit assistance, and construction administration. We tailor our services to meet your specific project needs.

How do you approach sustainable design?

Sustainability is core to our design philosophy. We prioritize energy efficiency, sustainable materials, water conservation, and indoor air quality. Many of our projects achieve LEED certification or similar green building standards.

What's your fee structure?

We typically work on a fixed fee basis determined by project scope. For smaller projects or consultations, we offer hourly rates. We provide detailed proposals outlining all costs upfront with no hidden fees.

Can you help with permit applications?

Absolutely. We assist with all necessary permits and approvals. Our team has extensive experience navigating local building codes and zoning requirements to ensure smooth project approval.
