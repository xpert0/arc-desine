Frequently Asked Questions

What's the typical timeline for a design project?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Residential projects typically take 3-6 months for design development, while commercial projects may require 6-12 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work on projects outside New York?

Yes, we take on select projects nationwide and internationally. For projects outside the NYC area, we collaborate with local contractors and may charge additional travel expenses for site visits.

What's included in your design services?

Our comprehensive services include concept development, brand strategy, visual design, UI/UX prototyping, user testing, design systems, and implementation support. We tailor our services to meet your specific project needs and brand objectives.

How do you approach user experience design?

User experience is central to our design philosophy. We prioritize user research, accessibility, intuitive navigation, and conversion optimization. Our designs are tested and refined to ensure they deliver exceptional user experiences across all devices.

What's your fee structure?

We typically work on a fixed project fee based on scope and deliverables. For ongoing work or consultations, we offer retainer arrangements and hourly rates. We provide detailed proposals outlining all costs upfront with no hidden fees.

Do you provide brand guidelines and assets?

Absolutely. We deliver complete brand guidelines, style sheets, design systems, and all source files. Our team ensures you have everything needed for consistent brand implementation across all touchpoints and future projects.
